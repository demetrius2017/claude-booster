## Problems / Solutions

The central risk was treating a textual “Fable answered” claim as evidence.
That would let a caller forge provenance and silently consume the decision slot.
The fix was to make the trusted, canonical runner perform the consultation and
bind its own receipt to the atomic project-local state transition. Audit findings
also tightened policy boundaries and lifecycle/recovery behavior before the final
fifth audit passed.
