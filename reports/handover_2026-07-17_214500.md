1. **Autopilot CADENCE class** (`a5249c8`) ‚Äî pure timing/sequencing questions

   after restart and proceeded autonomously.
3. **GLM/zai empty-response resilience** (`3319147`) ‚Äî GLM works; transient Z.ai
   empty responses were being logged `success=1` (telemetry recorded
   `success=returncode==0`, ignoring empty stdout), which poisoned
   `model_balancer._query_provider_health` and blocked demotion. Fix: binary
   stdout capture (byte-fidelity), retry-once on empty-exit-0
   (`ZAI_EMPTY_RETRY_BACKOFF_S`, default 0.5), honest
   `success=(exit0 AND non-empty)`, `return 1` on persistent empty.

Cross-provider design review earned its keep twice: the Opus Challenge caught a
`text=True` byte-fidelity contradiction in the zai PFD *before code*, and the
Opus diff-review confirmed the autopilot MED (over-broad `_is_cadence`) fixed
inline.
