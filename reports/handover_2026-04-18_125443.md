### C11 — telemetry_agent_health.py (~1h, ✅)

**New file:** `~/.claude/scripts/telemetry_agent_health.py` (~300 LOC).

Reads `<project>/reports/handover_*.md` (newest 10 by mtime) + `rolling_memory.db` read-only.

**Five signals** (consilium §Q3 Implementation #2):

| # | Signal | Method | Target | Note |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | Evidence density | Regex match count of `curl\|psql\|sqlite3\|SELECT\|docker\|kubectl\|DevTools\|Screenshot\|HTTP/\|exit=\|PRAGMA\|npm run\|pnpm\|wget\|pytest` (case-insensitive) across handover texts | ≥80% of files (ceil) | Corresponds to consilium "evidence artifacts" KPI |
| 2 | N/A ratio | Find `Verification\|Evidence\|...` heading, slice to next heading, check for `N/A\|not applicable\|nothing to verify\|skipped` | <30% of *verified* handovers | Denominator excludes handovers with no verification section |
| 3 | Overdue [UNDER REVIEW] tags | DB: `SELECT COUNT(*) WHERE status='under_review' AND resolve_by_date < today` | 0 | Q1 source-of-truth |
| 4 | Stale citations | DB `superseded` rows × handover text (substring-scan of full source path) | 0 cited | Gap until Q2 starts producing superseded rows |
| 5 | Session cadence | Count handover files dated in window (default 30d) | 1-10 "steady"; 0 "stagnant"; >10 "thrashing" | Substituted for "topic repetition streak" per v1 docstring limitation |

**Modes:**
- Prose (default): `=== AGENT HEALTH …` block with ✓/⚠ markers.
- JSON (`--json`): `{cmd, project, window_days, handovers_inspected, signals: {…}, exit_status}`.
- Always exits 0 — informational, not blocking.

|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | HIGH | Cadence never triggers "thrashing" — `--limit 10` caps handover count at 10, but cadence target is >10 | Split: load ALL handovers for cadence, truncate to `--limit` for text signals (evidence, N/A, stale citations). | 2 | MEDIUM | Evidence threshold uses `int(total*0.8)` → 4/6 passes as ≥80% | Use `math.ceil`. Now total=6 requires 5; total=10 requires 8. Verified. |
| 3 | MEDIUM | N/A ratio denominator includes handovers with no verification section | Separate `with_verification` denominator; handovers without `Verification` heading excluded. Now "0/5 verified" instead of "0/10" — more honest. |
| 4 | MEDIUM | 2000-char slice for verification body can miss long sections / leak into next section | Cut at next Markdown heading (`##+` or `**bold**`) via `_NEXT_HEADING` regex. |
| 5 | MEDIUM | Evidence markers case-sensitive (`SELECT` vs `select`) | Precompiled with `re.IGNORECASE`. Added `wget` and `pytest` per GPT suggestion. |
| 6 | LOW | Prose mode hid DB errors behind `-1` sentinel | Surface `"DB error: <msg>"` in prose when `error` field set. |
| 7 | LOW | `--json` help text promised uniform envelope but implementation is mixed | Updated help: "cmd key + subcommand-specific keys; start-context/similar curated, search/list raw". |
| 8 | LOW (deferred) | Prose start-context duplicates title extraction vs `_start_context_title()` | Kept — low drift risk for v1, refactor candidate for Q2 commit. |
