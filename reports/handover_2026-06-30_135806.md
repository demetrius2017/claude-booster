## Problems / Solutions

**Solution:** `/go` now has a mandatory Prototype Gate for broker/data/DB/financial/migration/external-system/incident/critical work. The Prototyper must use read-only probes or notebooks to prove why the prototype and production code diverge before Worker edits begin.
